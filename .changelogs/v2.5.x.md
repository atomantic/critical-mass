## v2.5.6 - Galaxy Tier, 3D Visualization Enhancements

Released: 2026-02-08

### Added

- New `galaxy` tier (🌌) between hypergiant and black_hole (1000-5000× base mass)
- 3D: black hole renders stationary at center with accretion disk + gravitational glow
- 3D: stellar tiers (sun, hypergiant, galaxy) use bright MeshBasicMaterial core that triggers bloom
- 3D: bloom post-processing via @react-three/postprocessing for natural glow effects
- 3D: dual-layer starfield with fog depth for visual depth
- 3D: pinned tooltip stays on last-hovered body (doesn't disappear when body orbits away)

### Changed

- Black hole minMass raised from 1000× to 5000× to accommodate galaxy tier
- 7 tiers: satellite(1×) → moon(3×) → planet(10×) → sun(100×) → hypergiant(500×) → galaxy(1000×) → black_hole(5000×)
- Stellar bodies render with bloom-driven glow instead of stacked transparent spheres

## v2.5.5 - Celestial Recovery, Buy→TP Tracking & Dashboard Enhancements

Released: 2026-02-08

### Added

- Buy→TP tracking: celestial bodies now track constituent buy orders via `buyOrders` array
- Dashboard: celestial bodies show buy count, hover to reveal buy→TP associations
- Dashboard: tier-specific colors and tooltips for open order types (entry, satellite→black_hole)
- 3D celestial visualization: Three.js orbital scene with tier-colored bodies, rings, incoming orders
- Recovery safety net: creates recovery body for position BTC not tracked by any celestial body
- Startup: bodies with missing TP orders automatically get TPs placed

### Changed

- Celestial tier thresholds scaled exponentially: satellite(1×) moon(3×) planet(10×) sun(100×) hypergiant(500×) black_hole(1000×)
- Tier proximity thresholds widened for larger tiers (0.5%→4.0%)

### Fixed

- TP% cap: holdback floor no longer bypasses effectiveMax, preventing astronomical TP prices on small bodies
- Startup reprice: overpriced body TPs detected and repriced on engine restart
- Orphan reprice: reclaimed orphan bodies also checked for overpriced TPs
- Reprice boundary: 1% epsilon prevents unnecessary cancel/replace for TPs at the exact max
- Recovery: bodies with null tpOrderId in saved state now get TP orders placed (previously skipped)
- Race condition: dedup guard prevents double-processing when stale check and fill check detect same fill simultaneously
- Orphan reclamation: sell orders priced at or below avg cost now cancelled instead of reclaimed as broken bodies
